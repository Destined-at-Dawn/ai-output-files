# 2026-06-02 Memory

## FPGA EEPROM/ADC Customer Handoff Closeout

- Completed EEPROM AT21CS01 warm-reset fix after boss confirmed cold download
  passed but push-button reset failed.
- Root cause recorded: AT21CS01 may remain in standard-speed mode after a pass,
  and the board powers the part from DQ. Short high-speed reset was not a safe
  warm reset.
- Final EEPROM fix: DQ high recharge wait 50 ms, reset low 600 us,
  reset-release high 200 us, smaller ILA depth, last_fail_state/addr probes.
